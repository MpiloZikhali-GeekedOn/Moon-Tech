Frequently Asked Questions

What is a stokvel?

A stokvel is a traditional South African community savings group where members contribute money regularly and receive payouts on a rotating basis. Stokvel Sami modernizes this concept with digital tools and additional benefits.

How much does it cost to use Stokvel Sami?

Basic stokvel management is free. We offer premium features like "Grow with Sami" investment options for a small fee, but the core functionality is completely free to use.

Is my money safe with Stokvel Sami?

Yes, we prioritize security and transparency. All transactions are recorded, and members can see exactly where money is allocated. We use bank-level security protocols to protect your information.

Can I join multiple stokvels?

Absolutely! You can participate in multiple stokvels for different purposes - one for grocery savings, another for property investment, etc.

How does the AI shopping assistant work?

Our AI assistant, Mpilo, analyzes your budget and preferences to recommend products that offer the best value for your money. It learns from your choices to provide increasingly better recommendations.
